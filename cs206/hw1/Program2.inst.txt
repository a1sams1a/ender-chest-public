Instruction of Program2
1. Choose operation. 1 is for addition, 2 is for multiplication, 3 is for differentation.
2. Addition and multiplication will require 2 polynomials and differentation will require 1 polynomial.
3. Format and restrictions of entering polynomial is same as program1.
4. Program will print out polynomial(s) that you entered and the result of the operation that you choose.
5. If you want to exit, enter q or Q when choose operations.
